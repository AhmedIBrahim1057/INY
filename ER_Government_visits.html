project info.

Project Name
ER Government visits - Abu Dhabi
Project Scope
Design – Implementation

Staff Major General Sheikh Ahmed bin Tahnoun was briefed on UAE National Railway Network’s freight operations during a visit to Etihad Rail’s main operations facility in Al Faya, Abu Dhabi, which included a journey aboard a prototype passenger train from Al Faya to Al Qudra in Dubai. The event was a resounding success, showcasing the strength of our collaboration. Together, we organized a seamless and impactful event that not only met but exceeded expectations. Through careful planning, clear communication, and mutual dedication, we ensured every detail was executed flawlessly. The event created opportunities for networking, engagement, and demonstrated the value of our partnership. Feedback from attendees was overwhelmingly positive, highlighting the professionalism and creativity both teams brought to the table. This successful event has further strengthened our relationship, setting the stage for future collaboration and continued success.
